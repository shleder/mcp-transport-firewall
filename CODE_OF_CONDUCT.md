

This project exists to improve the safety of open MCP infrastructure. We expect discussions, review, and collaboration to stay technical, respectful, and evidence-driven.

Participants are expected to:

- engage in good-faith technical discussion
- challenge ideas without attacking people
- share security findings responsibly
- avoid harassment, intimidation, doxxing, or abusive language
- keep public reports free of sensitive exploit details when active risk exists


This applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, release threads, and other project-managed spaces.


Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.


For conduct issues, open a private maintainer contact thread if available. If private contact is unavailable, open a minimal issue requesting maintainer follow-up without posting personal or sensitive details.
